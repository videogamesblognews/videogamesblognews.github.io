Top-selling Spanish video games for July 2022, as reported by AEVI: Winner: Xenoblade Chronicles 3

According to AEVI, these will be the best-selling games in Spain for the month of July, 2022. On the list of best-selling games, Xenoblade Chronicles 3, Switch Sports, and Live A Live take the top three spots.

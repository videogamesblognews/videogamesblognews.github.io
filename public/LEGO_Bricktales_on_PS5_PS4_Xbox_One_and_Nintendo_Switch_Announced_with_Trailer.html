LEGO Bricktales on PS5, PS4, Xbox One, and Nintendo Switch Announced with Trailer

LEGO Bricktales was announced with a trailer for the PlayStation 5, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and Nintendo Switch. Playstation 5, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Xbox One X, and Nintendo Switch will all be able to play LEGO Bricktales when it launches later this year.

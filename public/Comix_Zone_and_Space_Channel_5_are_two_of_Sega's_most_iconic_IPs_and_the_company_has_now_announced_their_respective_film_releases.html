Comix Zone and Space Channel 5 are two of Sega's most iconic IPs, and the company has now announced their respective film releases.

Film adaptations of Sega's Comix Zone and Space Channel 5 IPs have been announced. Sega has announced that two movies based on Space Channel 5 and Comix Zone would be made.

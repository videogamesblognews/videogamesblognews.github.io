Your Splatoon 2 save data will provide you access to exclusive bonuses in Splatoon 3.

If the incredible details revealed at the Splatoon Direct weren't enough, Nintendo has added a thin layer (of paint) by confirming that players may take their Splatoon 2 save data with them to their Switch accounts.

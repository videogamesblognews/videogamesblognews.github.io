LaLiga will be renamed EA Sports' LaLiga starting in the 2023/24 season: FIFA's creators will invest in the Spanish leagues of Primera and Segunda.

As with the 2023/2024 season, LaLiga will go by a new name and announce a partnership with EA Sports, the largest investor in Spanish football.

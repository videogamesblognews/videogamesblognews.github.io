Do we get smarter if we're good at video games? Psychologists are looking at it as a possible replacement for traditional intelligence testing.

Academic research has begun to use video games as a substitute for traditional intelligence exams; if you're very skilled at League of Legends or Minecraft, you may be interested in having your cognitive ability measured.

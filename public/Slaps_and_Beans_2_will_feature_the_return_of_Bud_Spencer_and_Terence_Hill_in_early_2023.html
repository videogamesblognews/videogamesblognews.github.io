Slaps and Beans 2 will feature the return of Bud Spencer and Terence Hill in early 2023.

After an absence of more than five years since the release of the first beat 'em up based on the iconic duo of action comedies, Bud Spencer and Terence Hill will return at the beginning of the next year.

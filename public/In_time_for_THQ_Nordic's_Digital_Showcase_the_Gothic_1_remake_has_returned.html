In time for THQ Nordic's Digital Showcase, the Gothic 1 remake has returned.

THQ Nordic is planning to re-release Gothic, a role-playing game (RPG) developed by Piranha Bytes and first released in 2001, thanks to one of the many licenses acquired over the years by Embracer.

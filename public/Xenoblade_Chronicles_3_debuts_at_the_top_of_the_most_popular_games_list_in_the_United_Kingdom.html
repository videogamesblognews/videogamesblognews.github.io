Xenoblade Chronicles 3 debuts at the top of the most popular games list in the United Kingdom.

In the United Kingdom's official chart, Xenoblade Chronicles 3 debuts at number one among the most popular games. GfK has delivered the sales data needed to compile the list of the most popular games.

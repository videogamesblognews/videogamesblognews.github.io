In the United States in July of 2022, the PlayStation 5 outsold the Nintendo Switch in terms of revenue but not sales.

According to NPD Group, the PlayStation 5 will outsell the Nintendo Switch in terms of revenue but fall short in terms of sales volume in the United States by the end of the year 2022. According to NPD Group data, PS5 was the best-selling system in 2014.

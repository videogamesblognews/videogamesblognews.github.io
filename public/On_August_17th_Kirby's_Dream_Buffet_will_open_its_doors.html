On August 17th, Kirby's Dream Buffet will open its doors.

Kirby's Dream Buffet, which had been delayed for a few weeks but was announced at the end of the month, would finally be released on August 17th, as was revealed during a tribute event honoring Kirby's 30th anniversary.

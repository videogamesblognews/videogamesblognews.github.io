Moonbreaker, a science-fiction tactical game published by Unknown Worlds, will be shown at Gamescom 2022 (gc2022).

Therefore, Unknown Worlds will follow Subnautica with Moonbreaker, a turn-based strategy game in the vein of Warhammer whose concept is to retranslate the exploration and discovery of Subnautica.

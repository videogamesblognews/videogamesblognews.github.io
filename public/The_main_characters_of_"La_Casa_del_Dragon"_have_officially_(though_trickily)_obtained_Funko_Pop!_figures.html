The main characters of "La Casa del Dragón" have officially (though trickily) obtained Funko Pop! figures.

To celebrate the release of La Casa del Dragón, the official spin-off of Game of Thrones, now available on HBO Max, you can pre-order Funko Pop! figures of the show's protagonists.
